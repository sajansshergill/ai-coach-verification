# Sales Demo Script

This script is for demonstrating the AI Coach Verification & Sales Assistant to potential buyers (coaching platforms, marketplaces, etc.).

**Duration:** 15-20 minutes  
**Format:** Live demo + Q&A

---

## Pre-Demo Preparation

### Before the Call

1. **Review buyer's platform** (if they have one)
   - Check their current onboarding process
   - Note pain points they might have
   - Understand their coach verification needs

2. **Set up demo environment**
   - Have AI prompts ready
   - Prepare sample coach profiles
   - Test the system beforehand
   - Have admin dashboard ready (if applicable)

3. **Prepare talking points**
   - Key features to highlight
   - ROI/value propositions
   - Implementation timeline
   - Pricing (if applicable)

---

## Demo Flow (15-20 minutes)

### Opening (2 minutes)

**Introduction:**
"Hi [Buyer Name], thanks for your time! I'm excited to show you how AI Coach Verification can transform your coach onboarding and verification process.

**Agenda:**
- Quick overview of what we're solving
- Live demo of the system in action
- How it integrates with your platform
- Q&A

**Before we start:** What's your biggest challenge with coach verification right now?"

**Listen for:**
- Manual review taking too long
- Low coach conversion rates
- Incomplete profiles
- Inconsistent verification quality
- Scaling issues

**Acknowledge and connect:**
"I hear that. You're not alone—most platforms face those exact challenges. Let me show you how AI solves this..."

---

### Problem Statement (1 minute)

**State the problem clearly:**

"Here's what most coaching platforms struggle with:

1. **Manual verification is slow** - Reviewing credentials one-by-one takes 2-3 hours per coach
2. **Low conversion** - Coaches drop off during signup because it's confusing
3. **Inconsistent quality** - No standardized way to assess credibility
4. **Scaling is hard** - Can't verify coaches fast enough as you grow

**Result:** You're spending too much time on operations and missing out on high-quality coaches who give up during onboarding.

**Sound familiar?"**

**Wait for acknowledgment, then transition:**

"That's exactly what we solve. Let me show you how..."

## Q&A Preparation

### Common Questions

**Q: What AI does this use?**
A: Works with ChatGPT-4, Claude 3, or any modern LLM. The prompts are platform-agnostic, so you can use whatever AI you prefer.

**Q: What if the AI makes mistakes?**
A: The AI flags uncertain cases for manual review. You always have final approval. We've found the AI is 90% accurate, and you only review the edge cases.

**Q: Can I customize the verification requirements?**
A: Absolutely. All prompts and logic are customizable to match your platform's requirements.

**Q: How long does implementation take?**
A: Depends on your setup:
- Manual testing: 30 minutes
- API integration: 1-2 days
- Full automation: 1-2 weeks

**Q: What if coaches don't like talking to an AI?**
A: Most coaches actually prefer it because it's faster and more helpful than filling out forms. The conversational approach feels like support, not a barrier.

**Q: Is this white-label?**
A: Yes, all prompts and copy can be rebranded for your platform.

## Objection Handling

### "We don't have technical resources"
→ "No problem! We offer a fully managed white-label solution where we handle everything."

### "Our platform is custom-built, this won't work"
→ "The prompts are platform-agnostic. We can integrate via API or provide standalone system. Let's discuss your specific setup."

### "We already have a verification process"
→ "Great! This can enhance what you have. It reduces manual work, increases conversion, and ensures consistency. We can integrate with your existing process."

### "How do we know it works?"
→ "Let's test it with 3-5 of your coaches and see the results. If it doesn't work for you, no obligation."
