## Key Observations

### What Makes Good Summaries

✅ **Specific numbers** - "5 years", "over 40 clients", "35% increase"  
✅ **Clear credentials** - "ICF-certified", "Ph.D. in Psychology"  
✅ **Concrete results** - "increased revenue by 40%", "promoted to CTO"  
✅ **Relevant context** - "Fortune 500 tech companies", "mid-career professionals"  
✅ **Trust signals** - Payment verification, testimonials, certifications

### What to Avoid

❌ **Generic fluff** - "passionate coach", "dedicated professional"  
❌ **Vague claims** - "helps clients succeed" (how?)  
❌ **Unverified claims** - Results without proof  
❌ **Too long** - Keep to 2-3 paragraphs (150-250 words)

## Customization Tips

### For Different Niches

**Executive Coaching:**
- Emphasize leadership experience
- Highlight promotion/advancement results
- Mention company sizes/industries

**Career Coaching:**
- Focus on transition success stories
- Highlight job search results
- Emphasize clarity and direction

**Life Coaching:**
- Emphasize transformation and well-being
- Highlight work-life balance results
- Focus on holistic approach

**Business Coaching:**
- Emphasize revenue/business growth
- Highlight financial metrics
- Focus on scaling and operations
